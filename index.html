Why Sleep Matters
7–9 hours — Optimal for muscle recovery and fat loss
6–7 hours — Reduced recovery, higher cortisol
Under 6 hours — Impairs gains, increases hunger hormones
Tips for better sleep:
• Set a consistent bed and wake time
• Avoid screens 1 hour before bed
• Keep your room cool and dark
• Avoid caffeine after 2pm
• No heavy meals within 2 hours of bedtime
